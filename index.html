Frequently Asked Questions

Is Listify free to use?

Yes! Every feature of Listify is free, and we are committed to keeping it that way. Our goal is to offer a tool that everyone can use without any cost. All future features that we add will also be free, and Listify will never ask you to pay.

Do I need to download an app, or can I use it in my browser?

Listify is a web-based tool, so you can use it directly in your browser without needing to download anything. While there is no mobile app currently available, one may be introduced in the future. We are working every day to make Listify more accessible and broaden its reach.

Can I collaborate with my team?

Absolutely! Listify allows you to assign tasks and work together efficiently. You can add team members to different projects, so while all are part of the team, only relevant members are assigned to specific projects. You can also assign members to specific tasks, ensuring smooth collaboration. We are continuously improving features to make teamwork even easier and more effective.

How do I sign up?

To sign up, simply click the 'Start for Free' button, log in with Google, and create an account in seconds. It's quick and easy, so you can start using Listify right away!
